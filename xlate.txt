   Translations for lunar calendars.  Adding further Latin-alphabet languages
   should be quite easy.  Each begins with '!' followed by a letter for that
   language,  followed by a line with the abbreviated days of the week,  followed
   by a line with the month initials for the top line.
   Cyrillic or Asian text is a job for another day (sadly,  PostScript is
   surprisingly ignorant of Unicode...)
